# Lookup Creative Work

**Step 1**: Identify the work title (film, book, song, album) in the question.
**Step 2**: Retrieve the work's Wikipedia entry.
**Step 3**: Extract the relevant production detail:
- For director: look for "directed by [name]"
- For screenwriter/author: look for "written by" or "screenplay by"
- For producer: look for "produced by"
- For composer: look for "music by" or "composed by"
- For genre: look for genre tags in the opening description (e.g. "is a 2003 action film")
